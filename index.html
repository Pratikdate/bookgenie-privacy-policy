3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell your personal data. However, we may share your data:

We implement industry-standard encryption, firewalls, and secure server technologies to protect your data. However, no digital system is 100% secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and update them regularly.

5. Children’s Privacy

BookGenie is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
